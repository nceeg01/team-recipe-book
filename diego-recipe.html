Classic Mac and Cheese

By: Diego Vasquez

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil macaroni in salted water according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to form a roux and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth and thickened.
  4. Add shredded cheese, salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir until cheese is melted and sauce is creamy.
  5. Mix cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce until fully coated.
  6. Optional: Pour into a baking dish, top with extra cheese, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 15 minutes.

Cooking time: 30 minutes

Serves: 4 people
