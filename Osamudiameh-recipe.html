Shawarma

By: Chris Okungbowa

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken strips
  • Pita bread
  • Shredded cabbage and lettuce
  • Ketchup and mayo
  • Sliced peppers and onions

Instructions:

  1. Grill or pan-fry the chicken until golden.
  2. Warm the pita bread slightly.
  3. Layer the vegetables, chicken, and sauces inside the pita.
  4. Wrap tightly and serve warm.

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Serves: 2-people
